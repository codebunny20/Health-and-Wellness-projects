Welcome to TAWT

A private, local, no‑signup tracker for daily wellness, transition milestones, and cycle patterns.

TAWT helps you notice trends, honor progress, and keep gentle tabs on mood, energy, sleep, medication/HRT adherence, and key transition moments — all stored only in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. You stay in control.

Highlights

  • Daily wellness: mood, energy, sleep, meds/HRT + notes.
  • Milestones timeline: record affirming steps and dates.
  • Cycle tracker: start/end, flow, notes, predicted next start.
  • Reports: rolling averages, adherence, variability, sparklines.
  • Privacy: 100% localStorage. Export/Import JSON backups.
  • Accessible: keyboard friendly, clear labels, low noise UI.

Getting started

  1. Open Track and save today’s entry.
  2. Add a recent or historical milestone.
  3. Visit Reports after a few days to see trends.
  4. Export periodically for a personal backup.

You can clear or restore data anytime. Everything stays on your device unless you export it.
